## What is Jekyll?

Jekyll is a simple, static site generator that transforms your plain text into beautiful websites and blogs. It's perfect for:

- **Fast Performance** - Static HTML means lightning-quick load times
- **Security** - No database vulnerabilities or server-side code to worry about
- **Easy Hosting** - Seamlessly deploy to GitHub Pages for free
- **Version Control** - Keep your entire site in Git
